# campus-complaint-system
A full-stack web application to streamline the registration, tracking, and resolution of maintenance complaints on a campus

# Title
Campus Maintenance & Complaint Management System

# Problem Statement
In large educational campuses, students and staff frequently encounter maintenance-related issues such as electrical failures, plumbing leaks, network outages, and classroom infrastructure problems. These issues are often reported through informal channels like phone calls, WhatsApp messages, or verbal communication, resulting in incomplete records, delayed resolutions, repeated complaints, and lack of accountability.
The Campus Maintenance & Complaint Management System is a full-stack web-based application designed to digitize and streamline the complaint handling process. The system allows users to raise maintenance complaints, enables maintenance staff to manage and resolve issues efficiently, and provides administrators with centralized visibility into complaint status, response time, and operational efficiency.
This platform improves transparency, accountability, and response time by maintaining a structured workflow and real-time tracking of complaints.
